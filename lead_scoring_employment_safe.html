Business Challenge

Our company launched a high-ticket B2B program but was experiencing zero conversions despite significant marketing effort across multiple channels. With 426 potential leads from various customer segments and leadership unavailable for guidance, I took initiative to develop a systematic approach to identify and prioritize the highest-probability prospects for personalized outreach.

Key Constraints

  • CRM lead scoring capability not activated
  • Limited time for decision-making during critical launch period
  • Need to protect perceived value of premium program offerings
  • Founder's time required strategic allocation for maximum impact

Methodology & AI-Assisted Development

I partnered with ChatGPT to develop a sophisticated 8-factor scoring system that balanced customer engagement indicators with strategic business considerations. The methodology prioritized recent engagement patterns, customer satisfaction signals, and communication accessibility levels.

Critical Business Intelligence Uncovered

  • 📧

    Email Deliverability Crisis Identified

    Most leads had never received program emails despite multiple campaigns, revealing massive database health issues requiring immediate attention

  • 📅

    Market Timing Intelligence

    Customer feedback revealed poor seasonal timing, with optimal launch window being early spring vs. busy summer season

  • 🔄

    Process Improvement Opportunities

    Identified lack of systematic market research before campaign launches, connecting poor performance to process deficiencies

Strategic Impact & Business Value

Long-term Business Improvements

The project evolved beyond lead scoring to become a comprehensive business intelligence initiative. Database health improvement strategies were implemented, seasonal timing insights influenced future planning, and systematic process improvements were recommended for campaign development.

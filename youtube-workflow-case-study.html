The Challenge

When I joined the team, video production was basic: the founder shot content on his phone while walking around the house, using free apps with watermarked editing, or having his high school son do editing on a laptop. Sound quality was poor, video was inconsistent, and editing was elementary.

The Operational Reality: We had missed deadlines, no continuous recording or release schedule, and content was released whenever we had something ready. There was no consistency in release timing or production standards. We were working with local photographers and videographers, but the overall process needed systematization.

Core Problems to Solve:

  • Inconsistent content release schedule—sometimes weekly, sometimes monthly
  • Poor production quality undermining professional credibility
  • Ad-hoc workflows that couldn't scale with business growth
  • Lack of team coordination and clear responsibilities

Strategic Solution

I developed a comprehensive workflow system that transformed content creation into a systematic, professional operation through systematic process development, strategic tool integration, and team coordination across skill levels.

🎯 Systematic Process Development

Created a 6-phase workflow covering content planning, production, post-production, review, and distribution. Implemented quarterly shoot scheduling, condensing recording into 2 intensive days per quarter.

⚙️ Tool Integration Strategy

Selected and integrated Trello for visual project management, Frame.io for professional video asset management, ChatGPT for brand-consistent content creation, and Buffer for strategic content distribution.

👥 Team Coordination

Managed contractors with varying technical comfort levels. Trained distributed team across multiple countries to use systematic project management instead of ad-hoc communication.

📅 Quarterly Planning Model

Established quarterly planning structure that provides predictable production cycles while giving creative team systematic content to work with throughout the quarter.

Tool Selection & Integration

Strategic tool selection based on team capabilities, workflow requirements, and scalability needs:

Trello

Selected for its visual, uncluttered interface that works across diverse team skills and international contractors. Handles project tracking and team coordination.

Frame.io

Adopted at editor team's recommendation, now preferred platform for professional video asset management and review workflows.

ChatGPT

First AI implementation in the company. Trained the system on brand voice and messaging for consistent content creation across all materials.

Buffer

Dual functionality for both scheduled posts and drip campaign content distribution. Cost-effective solution with reliable performance.

Implementation Journey

This wasn't a quick implementation but rather a continuous improvement process spanning multiple years. Initially, there was some resistance to change, requiring me to work within existing preferences.

Years 1-3: Foundation Building

Gradual implementation of basic workflows and tool adoption while working within existing preferences and comfort levels.

Years 4-5: Increased Control

Gained increasing authority over process decisions, enabling more significant systematic improvements and team training.

Recent Breakthrough: Quarterly Scheduling

Implemented quarterly shoot schedules—two intensive shooting days provide complete freedom for the next three months while giving team systematic content.

Change Management Success: The key breakthrough came when I implemented quarterly shoot schedules. There was initial hesitation, but this approach is now preferred—two intensive shooting days provide complete freedom for the next three months, while giving the team systematic content to work with.

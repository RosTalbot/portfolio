Crisis Context & Leadership Initiative

Our 14-year contractor coaching company was in major financial trouble and desperately needed a cash injection. The launch of our $6,790 high-ticket sales mastermind program (targeting 7-10 sales for $47,530-$67,900 in revenue) was failing with zero conversions despite significant marketing investment. With our CMO on vacation during this critical period, I took initiative to develop a systematic data science approach to salvage the campaign.

High-Stakes Business Context

  • Company survival: Business in major financial crisis requiring immediate revenue
  • Premium program protection: War Room members ($14K/year, contractors making $5M+) needed careful handling
  • Leadership gap: CMO unavailable during critical campaign period
  • Resource constraints: CRM lead scoring not activated, limited tools available
